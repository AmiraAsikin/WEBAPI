ABOUT US



KUPTM is an organisation built on trust, resilience, and blessings. KUPTM education has successfully produced graduates who are holistically trained to be knowledgeable and enterprising. Indeed, the enriching experiences gained through the authentic simulations provided in our training have equipped our graduates not only with academic-related knowledge but also with human skills that will give them an edge in the job market. The establishment of clubs and associations at KUPTM has also helped produce many graduates who are active and have a high leadership style. Therefore, this system was built to help many students at KUPTM register clubs and associations established at KUPTM easily and quickly.
